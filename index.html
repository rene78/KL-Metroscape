Some thoughts on urban planning in KL

Rene

I try to do most of my commutes in Kuala Lumpur by bike or public transport. Having experienced the challenges firsthand, I have summarized the three most pressing shortcomings in urban planning and suggested ways to overcome them. I hope that my articles can provide helpful and informative insights to others.
