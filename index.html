KL Cityscape

Some thoughts on urban planning in KL

Rene

I try to do most of my commutes in Kuala Lumpur by bike and public transport, and as someone who has experienced the challenges firsthand, I wrote some articles highlighting the shortcomings in urban planning and suggesting ways to overcome them. I hope they can be helpful and informative to others.
